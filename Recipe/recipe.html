Prep time: 10 mins

Cook time: 15 mins

Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk buttermilk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients gently.
  4. Heat a pan, add butter, and pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook the other side.
  6. Serve warm with syrup and fruits. ğŸ“
